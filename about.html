About Us

Karmiq is a positive approach to living. Inspired by ‘karman’, we help people take charge of their well-being, through simple steps. Small changes they can make to welcome a world of wellness in return. We believe it’s the small deeds that count. So be it picking up dry fruits for those odd hour cravings, cooking your meal with olive oil, or sipping on green tea when you take a break, we nudge you closer to making the healthier choice. How? By sourcing our special range of products from all over the world, to give you only the best! All so that you can add some good karma in your wellness journey.
